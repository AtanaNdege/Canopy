The Process

From Invoice to
Capital in Days

A streamlined, transparent process designed for businesses that can't afford to wait 30, 60 or 90 days for payment.

01

Submit Your Invoices

Upload invoices through our secure portal. We accept B2B invoices from creditworthy debtors across all industries. No minimum volume required.

02

We Verify & Approve

Our credit team reviews your debtors within 24 hours. We assess the creditworthiness of who owes you — your credit history matters less.

03

Receive Your Advance

Funds hit your account within 24–48 hours of approval. We advance up to 90% of the invoice face value immediately.

04

Debtor Pays, We Remit

When your client pays, we release the reserve (the remaining 10%) minus our factoring fee — no hidden charges, ever.

Who Qualifies

Eligibility Criteria

We work with businesses of all sizes. Here's what we look for.

Registered Business Entity

Ltd, Pty Ltd, LLC, partnership or sole trader with a valid business registration number and at least 3 months operating history.

B2B Invoices Only

Invoices must be raised against other businesses or government entities. Consumer (B2C) receivables are not eligible.

Unencumbered Receivables

Invoices must be free of prior liens, assignments or charges. A first-position security interest over receivables is required.

Goods or Services Delivered

Invoices must represent completed, undisputed work. Progress claims and pre-delivery invoices are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

Creditworthy Debtors

Debtors must have no material adverse credit events. We perform our own debtor due diligence at no cost to you.

Invoice Tenor ≤ 120 Days

Standard payment terms of Net 30, 60, or 90 days. Extended terms up to 120 days considered for approved non-recourse facilities.
